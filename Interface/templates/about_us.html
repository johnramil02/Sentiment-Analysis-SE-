Analyze Sentiments


Project SAWE

Project Sentiment Analysis With emojis is a Sentiment Analysis of Tagalog, English and Taglish Languages. It can also analyze sentiment with Emoji

This application is a current thesis development tool to prove the performance of Convolutional Neural Network in Sentiment Analysis in the mentioned languages and emoji. It used product reviews as its dataset.

This can be used in diffent fields but mainly be utilized business and research.

Research

This tool can be utilized in the field of research specially when there are sentiment based surveys or questionnaire to classify and summarize the sentiments

Business

This can be utilized in the field of business by classifying and summarizing text-based product reviews .
